About

Welcome to my creative corner! My name is Nicole, I am a junior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I am studying Advertising with minors in business and informatics. You may be wondering: what is the significance of the number 808, why is that the name of your site? In music, an 808 machine is a low bass frequency that is considered a staple in many genres because of its unique sounds and impact on the industry. So, I decided to use the 808 in my own way; use it as the name of the central passions of my world.

I am an avid music enthusiast, passionate about capturing some of my favorite moments during concerts and curating different playlists to express the various emotions I feel. This site was created to showcase these thoughts through my own work and album reviews. All records are unique in their own way, and by no means am I a certified reviewer; I am here to share my opinions on latest releases, my personal favorites, oldies, and hidden gems.

Another edition to this site is a look into my own work. Some of the programs that I have experience working with include Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Canva, and Figma.
